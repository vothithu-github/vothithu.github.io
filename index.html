Your limit is just my starting point

  • Lifestyle

I remember forever saying a saying like this: "What will happen to you all your life like this?" Sometimes we think that changing ourselves is so important that tomorrow starts, but tomorrow appointment tomorrow, then day after day lazily creating for us a life of banal mediocrity.

One day passes the distance between the talented people in the company and us one more distance; after ten years that gap was enough for us to follow them without sandals. Do you think their success is due to family background, superior flattery or some other reason you don't know?.

1. Envy only makes you stay still, even fall behind, while the lives of your envious friends get better and better. Smart people spend time learning, useless people only disparage.

2. Being unfair to yourself is a kind of fairness. The strong always uphold the natural choice, the right one exists, only the weak will cry for justice day by day.

3. Efforts will be very hard, sometimes even frustrating, without effort is more comfortable. What's better than watching TV bite melon seeds every day, but when others get the achievement, don't be impatient.

4. The best way to resolve all disturbing emotions is not to ask too much. Instead of getting frustrated when others don't help, why not try to win it with your own strength? Why should they help you?

5. You can't do it, it doesn't mean other people can't. Vision determines ambition, realizes how far you can go.

6. The lower the starting point, the more effort. If your starting point is not equal to others, the only way for you to overcome them is to attempt a hundred times a thousand times more than them.

7. The higher the salary, the less free time. Others have a lot of money and poise to make you feel admired, but did you know that when you lie on the bed gossiping they are working hard outside?

8. The lower the salary, the lower the ability to dominate life. Objectively speaking, how your ability, the world in your hands will be like that. You feel that your salary is too low because you are standing at an angle of yourself. If one day you become a boss, remember that day, you will be grateful to the boss for loving you so much.
